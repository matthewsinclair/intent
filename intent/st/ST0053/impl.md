## Notes

- `IN-AU-STYLE-003` (front-matter/objectives) deliberately stays in the author pack -- book/course IA, not shared prose hygiene. The author style tier is a single rule (a legal numbering gap).
- WP02 tier placement is by check-type (mechanical->style, judgment->craft), matching the author convention; the ratified D4 scope is unchanged.
- WP03: `critic-prose` resolves its discipline from config `languages` (author, content, or both) -- one critic serves every prose discipline (D3); `--lang` override for mixed projects.
- WP04: `prose` (the `IN-PR-*` base) is deliberately NOT a `lang init`-able discipline (no `templates/prose/`) -- it is a base other disciplines pull in, like `agnostic`.
- WP05: the two dispatch surfaces (`/in-review` per-language loop, `/in-session` fan-out) both treat author and content as first-class prose disciplines routing to the one `critic-prose`; the D7 no-code-critic exclusion generalised from "author-only" to "prose-only".
